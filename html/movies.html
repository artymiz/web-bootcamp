Anime Movies that Art really likes

My 3 favourite anime movies.

Maquia: When the Promised Flower Blooms

2018, P.A Works
A tear jerker that follows the mother son relationship between a race who can live hundreds of years and an adopted human boy.

Wolf Children: Ame and Yuki

2012, Studio Chizu
A bitter sweet tale about the coming of age for siblings who are half werewolf and half human.

My Neighbour Totoro

1988, Studio Ghibli
Super cute, a classic children's movie. Love the giant furry creature.
